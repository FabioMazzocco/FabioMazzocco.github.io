Sharing knowledge in a worldwide-spread team

Project information

  • Category: Information Management | Knowledge Sharing
  • Client: Robert Bosch GmbH
  • Project date: Apr 2019 - Sept 2019
  • Team size: 1
  • Role: Project Manager and Front-end Developer

[Protected images]
The team works with Italian clients worldwide, with different limitations and challenges. Given the geographical extent of the team, many members might have never met. It was important to share information to improve the service, starting from the contacts of the team members. That included technical knowledge, customer preferences, best practices, news, and others. There was already a platform to do so, but old and poorly organized for the changes in the team and the clients' requirements. By analyzing the old portal, the system limitations, and talking to the stakeholders - i.e., the team members worldwide - it was possible to craft a new portal, closer to the needs of the team and the client. Information became quickly accessible and forums to share ideas were created. Two guides concluded the project: a guide for the use and a guide for the maintenance of the portal.
