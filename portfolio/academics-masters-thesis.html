A framework for Public Infrastructure Construction Management

As the world changes, governments are chartering several infrastructure projects, and even infrastructure project portfolio. However, literature and professional organizations have not yet delivered a framework that can help practitioners. My Master's Thesis is a first attempt to create one and support project managers in delivering value for the community. Moreover, it is an attempt to start a movement of creation of infrastructure project management frameworks by scholars and professional organizations.
